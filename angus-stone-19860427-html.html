Angus Stone (Folk Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Angus Stone is an Australian folk singer-songwriter, record producer and audio engineer. He is one half of the musical sibling duo Angus & Julia Stone, with whom he has released four studio albums. His debut solo album, Smoking Gun, was released in April 2009 under the pseudonym Lady of the Sunshine, and reached the top 50 on the ARIA Albums Chart. His second solo album, Broken Brights, was released under his name on 13 July 2012 and peaked at number 2.
