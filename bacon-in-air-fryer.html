Can I cook bacon in an air fryer?

AIR fryers have quickly become very popular because of their cost effectiveness, speed, and health benefits.

But what exactly can you cook in an air fryer?

What is an air fryer?

Air fryers can bake and roast like regular ovens, however they cook food much more quickly and evenly.

This is because an air fryer has heating elements at the top of it, as well as a fan.

The hot air flows around the food, circulating quickly to make it crispy in no time.

The heating mechanism also ensures that the food becomes crisp without using any oil, making it a healthier alternative to deep frying.

Advertisement

Most air fryer compartments are dishwasher safe, meaning you will usually have less cleaning up to do after cooking with it.

Can I cook bacon in an air fryer?

Cooking bacon in an air fryer is encouraged.

It is recommended that you cook your bacon at 200 degrees.

If you are cooking streaky bacon, it will take between six and ten minutes depending on how crispy you want it.

Advertisement

If you prefer your streaky bacon just cooked, take it out of the air fryer after six minutes, however if you like it crispier then let it cook for nine or ten minutes.

Similarly, back bacon will take between four and eight minutes to cook in the air fryer, depending on how well-done you like it.

It is also important that your rashers do not overlap when they are cooking.

Is cooking bacon in an air fryer healthier than pan frying?

Cooking bacon in an air fryer is significantly healthier than frying it in a regular pan.

Advertisement

Most people tend to add oil or butter to their pans when they pan fry bacon, however this isn't necessary when cooking with an air fryer.

Also, the fat from the bacon falls off whilst it's cooking and is left at the bottom of your air fryer.

While this can make for extra cleaning, it means that you are not consuming the bacon fat, making it healthier.

What else can I cook in an air fryer?

One of the reasons that air fryer's are so popular is because they can cook just about anything.

Advertisement

You can cook fresh meats, vegetables, frozen food, deserts, and more.

Toasted sandwiches and pizzas are popular air fryer recipe choices on TikTok.

The air fryer can be used to cook side dishes, but also your main course.

It is even possible to cook steak in them.
