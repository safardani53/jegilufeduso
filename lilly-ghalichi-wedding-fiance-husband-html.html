A Look at Lilly Ghalichi'sExquisite Engagement Rings and Married Life

Former Shahs Of Sunset star Lilly Ghalichi has been engaged twice. Although her first engagement with American businessman Dhar Mann didn't materialize, her second engagement with entrepreneur Dara Mir led to marriage. 

The story of her marriage, however, comes second to the details of her exquisite engagement rings, and deservingly so.

First Engagement Ring Was Worth Over a Million

Ghalichi was in a long-term relationship with business entrepreneur Dhar Mann in the early 2010s.

The pair got engaged in April 2014. Mann proposed to the television star with her dream Chanel engagement ring. The oval halo ring with a big diamond was worth $1.2 million. 

Ghalichi took to Instagram to announce the engagement. Posting a picture of her ring, she stated she didn't know what was more romantic, whether she got her dream band or that she was engaged. 

Just months after the engagement, Ghalichi announced her split with Mann through an Instagram post uploaded on December 17, 2014. She posted a picture with the entrepreneur and disclosed that they had broken up.

In the caption, she didn't mention the reason behind the split but praised her ex-fiancé. She stated it was nice to be with a loving and honest person after numerous failed relationships. She also asserted that she would always cherish their time together. 

Her Second Diamond-Studded Engagement Ring 

Moving on, Ghalichi started dating entrepreneur Dara Mir in March 2016.

The couple lived only a floor apart in their building's lobby. They met when the 38-year-old went to the board of her apartment building to get approval for parking her Ferrari. Mir was a member of the foundation.

Things escalated quickly after a brief introduction and Mir proposed to the reality star in late August 2016 on the grounds of their future home in Bel-Air.

His mother chose the oval-cut diamond ring with tiny diamonds on the band for the engagement.

Ghalichi was on cloud nine for getting the antique piece from her partner. She shared with Bravo TV that she could not have asked for anything more.

Ghalichi Married Dara Mir in 2017

The Houston native married her fiancé on May 6, 2017, at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Beverly Hills. She donned a white Ryan & Walter-designed couture ball gown with a train veil, while her partner looked clad in a black tuxedo.

A year after the wedding, the pair welcomed their daughter, Alara Mir, on September 30, 2018.

Their marriage has undergone challenges over the years, with Ghalichi filing for divorce in August 2019 for irreconcilable differences. The pair reconciled soon, but in August 2020, the mother-of-one again filed documents to end her marriage. 

A month later, she again dismissed the petition and reunited with her husband for her daughter's second birthday. Since then, their marriage seems to sail smoothly.

On their fourth wedding anniversary, Ghalichi posted a picture with her husband and stated that staying in the marriage wasn't easy. She then contrasted her statement, saying that it was still worth fighting for.
