Little People, Big World Stars Vacation in Disneyland: See the Happy Pics!

They’re little people.

They reside in a big world.

But they just spent the weekend in a very special land, as evidenced by the fun and special photos below.

Matt Roloff has updated his Instgram account to include a number of photos of himself, his girlfriend Caryn Chandler, his son, his daughter-in-law and two of his grandkids at the Happiest Place on Earth.

Yup, folks: They went to Disneyland!

"A very Happy Place!" wrote Matt as a caption to one snapshot of himself and Chandler, later adding as a message alongside the more inclusive family portrait above:

That feeling of celebration you get when you get the random draw ticket to get on the newest ride at Disneyland!

As you can see, it sure looks like everyone is having a blast.

And it also looks like Caryn is fitting in quite well with everyone, which ought not to come as a surprise to anyone who has chronicled Matt’s life over the past couple years.

Unlike ex-wife Amy Roloff, Matt is not engaged. He and Caryn continue to take things relatively slow.

However, Chandler acts in almost way like a grandmother to little Jackson and young Lilah, spending plenty of time with these infants because she lives with Matt in both Arizona and Oregon.

For whatever reason, many Little People, Big World fans often try to stir up controversy when it comes to Caryn Chandler.

Granted, they were given some material last summer when Amy wrote in her memoir that Matt cheated on her with Chandler toward the end of their marriage.

Maybe not physically, Amy admitted, but she was very candid in talking about how close Matt grew to Caryn back in the day; back when she worked as the manager on Roloff Farms.

“I think when you start looking before you’re separated in marriage, that is hard,” Amy also said in this interview, expounding on her cheating allegation last year as follows:

“When you become involved or much more than just a friend, but you become more in a relationship than just say ‘Hey, hi friend, lets go out for coffees’ type of thing, I think that can be hard on the other person as well."

Amy and Matt were married for almost 30 years, from 1987 to 2016.

They share four kids and also a business and really do seem to be on strong terms, despite all that past turmoil.

Matt, for his part, took the high road and never really fired back at Amy after she made this charge.

The couple seems awfully content and, based on the pictures above, it also seems rather clear that Tori and Zach have embraced Chandler as a major part of their lives.

So now the question will once again be asked:

When will Matt propose?!?
