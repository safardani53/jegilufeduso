Did TJ die on Manifest?

NBC’s drama series Manifest has been airing on the network since 2018.

The show’s third season premiered on April 1, 2020 and has already been unraveling the mystery behind the passengers of flight 828.

Did TJ die on Manifest?

TJ Morrison appeared to have lost his life in a nightclub fire in season two, episode seven.

He sacrifices himself to protect his friend Olive from the flames before he could receive any help from Ben.

TJ’s body is presumably discovered by Michaela, as she finds someone, later revealed to be Isaiah, burnt to a crisp clutching on to Olive’s bracelet. 

However, it is then revealed that TJ took cover in some underground tunnels after helping Olive.

HIs injuries prevented him from climbing out and is found by Olive and Ben all dehydrated and barely hanging on.

TJ was able to survive the fire but it ravaged six people that were not able to escape in time.

What is Manifest?

Manifest is a mystery drama series about the homecoming of Montego flight 828 after a year.

It explores the lives of the Stone family and those who are mourning the loss of passengers.

A deeper mystery unfolds in the series, and some of the returned passengers realize they may be meant for something greater than imaginable.

The series has grown in popularity since its premiere in 2018.

Actress Luna Blaise who plays Olive dished to Popsugar about season three, saying: “It's hard to explain [this season] without giving a lot away, but every detail in this season is leading closer to everyone understanding what's happened [on flight 828].

"The callings are getting stronger. It's very action packed. It's so hard to explain because there's so much that happens that I feel like if I say anything, it's going to give it away.”
