Who is Key Glock?

Born on August 3, 1997, Key Glock - real name Markeyvius LaShun Cathey - is a Memphis native.

In 2017, the late Young Dolph signed Key, his cousin through marriage, to the Paper Route Empire label.

Key dropped his debut single, Racks Today, on January 25 of the same year.

His first mixtape was released in June of 2017.

Dolph and Key worked together on a joint album, Dum and Dummer, in 2019.

The pair went on to tour across Europe, bringing the show to the United States in 2020.

Following Dolph's untimely death in November of 2021, Key dropped a tribute song dedicated to him entitled Proud.
