Next is selling a 23 Forky costume for kids who love Toy Story 4 but parents say its giv

GOT a kid who's obsessed with Toy Story 4? Next is selling costumes of their favourite characters.

Children can now dress up as Buzz, Woody and Bo Peep - but we're not sure if you'll want them to don the retailer's Forky outfit.

  • Rubies Disney™ Toy Story 4 Forky fancy dress costume, £23 from Next - buy now

If you're yet to see the latest installment in the Toy Story franchise, Forky is the result of a craft project at toddler Bonnie's kindergarten.

Created out of a plastic spork, he has uneven googly eyes and pipe cleaner arms.

Next's costume consists of a black and white all-in-one, with a red print designed to resemble Forky's bendable limbs.

It comes with four masks, showing the character's various expressions.

The wonky faces are supposed to be cute - but unfortunately, people seem to be finding them quite unsettling over on Twitter.

One person wrote: "Look at this Forky costume. Have you ever seen anything more terrifying in your life?"

Another disturbed tweeter said: "Nightmare-inducing Forky costume."

"I've officially seen the devil," and: "This is what a serial killer would wear," read other messages.

One joked: "Imagine waking up and the first thing you see is your child hovering over you wearing this."

Think you can handle putting your little one in the get-up?

It'll set you back £23, and you can buy it in sizes 3-4 years, 5-6 years and 7-8 years.

To be fair, Halloween is only three months away.
