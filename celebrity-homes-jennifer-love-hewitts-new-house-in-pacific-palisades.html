Jennifer Love Hewitt Purchases Home in Pacific Palisades

Jennifer Love Hewitt has purchased a modern home in Pacific Palisades from Peter Koral, co-founder of 7 for all Mankind jeans.

The actress paid $3,250,000 for the 2,872 square foot, 4 bedroom, 4.5 bathroom contemporary home which sits on .13 acres above Temescal Canyon.

Built in 2007, the urban loft inspired home was designed by Santa Monica-based architect Melinda Gray and features an open floor plan which leads to a sleek outdoor living space with a spa-style pool and lounging area with a fire pit and flat-screen television.

The open concept center island kitchen has granite topped custom bamboo cabinetry and high quality commercial style appliances, including two ovens, two dishwashers, a six burner range and a built-in espresso maker.

The upstairs includes a library loft and a master suite with walk-in closet, street-side balcony with a fire pit and a custom tiled bathroom with separate soaking tub and steam shower.
