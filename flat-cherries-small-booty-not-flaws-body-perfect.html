I have flat cherries and a small booty but they're not flaws, my peach is cute and my body is perfec

A BODY positive woman has embraced her figure in a confident statement.

She may have flat cherries and a small bottom, but she doesn't see them as flaws because, in her eyes, her body is perfect.

Self-love advocate Clara Dao (@clara_dao) uses her online platform to help others feel better about their bodies because she loves them so much, according to her TikTok bio.

In a video, she was transparent about aspects of herself that others may see as flaws.

The redhead stood in her home and danced to the popular song I Love My Body by Korean singer HWASA.

Clara wore a white button-down shirt that was tied up to show off her slim stomach and a navy blue plaid skirt.

She said that she "doesn't fit into beauty standards" because of her flat cherry chest, small peach butt, and constantly bloated stomach.

She then explained that she overcomes them by realizing that they're not flaws and listed what made them so great.

Her flat bust allows her to sleep on her stomach comfortably and she doesn't have back pain.

"Bloating means I had a good meal and I'm just digesting," she wrote in a set of subtitles.

She added that small butts are cute and "easier to slide in small spaces."

"This woman deserves the world I swear to God," a fan praised.

Another TikTok user shared: "I love your videos. It makes me more confident about how I look."

"I'm trying to gain weight, but when I saw this video I realized that my body is already beautiful," another added.

Another fan chimed in: "You help me so much with my confidence! Tysm."
