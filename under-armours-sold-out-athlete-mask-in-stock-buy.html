The previously sold-out Under Armour athlete's face mask is back in stock

THE top-selling Under Armour face mask for athletes is now back in stock.

Great news for those who want to work out and wear a comfortable and moisture-blocking mouth covering.

Under Armour's face mask for athletes has proven to be extremely popular, previously selling out on both the brand's UK and US sites. You can get your hands on it now, but you'll have to be quick, as we're not sure how long the stock will last.

  • Under Armour face mask for athletes, £26 - buy here

The mask features a structured design to sit comfortably on your face while working out, and is available on the official Under Armour online store.

It has three layers: a spacer fabric featuring air pockets; open-cell foam that makes it "easy to breathe but hard for moisture and sweat to pass"; and UA iso-chill fabric with an anti-microbial treatment to combat bacteria.

Under Armour's face mask for athletes is designed to stay off your mouth and wick sweat away, but still allow air to flow easily.

The mask also has a soft adjustable nose bridge to match the shape of your face, and a strip of baselayer fabric for a comfortable fit under glasses. The inside fabric - featuring UA iso-Chill technology - is designed to disperse the heat next to your skin, so it keeps cool during use.
