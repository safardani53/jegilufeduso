Stephanie Rose Bongiovi, Daughter of Veteran Musican, Arrested for Drug Possession

Stephanie Rose Bongiovi, the 19-year old daughter of New Jersey rocker Jon Bon Jovi, was arrested at Hamilton College today.

According to the the Town of Kirkland Police Department, via TMZ, officers responded to a report that Stephanie had overdosed on heroin in her dorm room.

Upon arriving at the scene, the authorities found Stephanie alive, along with a “small quality” of heroin, marijuana and other drug paraphernalia scattered around the premises.

 

Stephanie, the artist’s only daughter and the oldest of his four kids, was arrested on three misdemeanor charges:

Possession of a controlled substance, possession of marijuana and criminally using drug paraphernalia.

She was released from custody this morning and ordered to appear at a future hearing.
