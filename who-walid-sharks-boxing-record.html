Meet Iraqi YouTuber-turned-boxer Walid Sharks

BOXER Walid Sharks is part of the huge all-star-studded Misfits Boxing Prime Card Event.

Sharks' highly anticipated rematch against Deen the Great is set to be an explosive bout.

Who is Walid Sharks?

Walid Muhesein, also known as Walid Sharks, is an online social media star-turned-boxer. 

He was born on April 15, 2004, in Syria.

With 441k followers on Instagram and over 550k followers on TikTok the boxer has quite the following already. 

Walid will return to the ring on October 14, 2023, to face rival Deen the Great as part of the must-watch Misfits Boxing Prime Card.

Walid and Deen the Great will feature as one of the 11 flights on the undercard, before the highly anticipated KSI showdown with Tommy Fury, and Logan Paul vs Dillon Danis gets underway. 

What is Walid Sharks boxing record?

Sharks has a boxing record of 3-1-0 with two KOs and one win by unanimous decision under his belt already. 

His last fight was in July 2023, in which he secured a win over Yuddygangtv also known as YouTuber Elijah Smith.

The boxer has only lost one fight in his career.

Did Walid Sharks beat Deen the Great?

Sharks only defeat was at the hands of Deen the Great, real name Nurideen Shabazz.

Their clash took place on November 19, 2022.

There were multiple knockdowns during the fight which was part of the Misfits Boxing X Series 003 event. 

Sharks looked dominant until the sudden finish in the third round when he was KO'd by Deen.

Nearly a year later, they will go head-to-head on the undercard of KSI vs Tommy Fury on October 14, 2023.

They have both notched wins since then, but with their long-lasting feud and rivalry, it leaves it open for Sharks to even up the series.

Sharks has never beaten Deen the Great.

The last time the pair met was in a Misfits Boxing tag team boxing experiment in July 2023.

Deen and Sharks were paired up as teammates, despite their well-known feud. 

The explosive duo took on Yuddygang TV and Ayye Pap in the co-main event of Misfits Boxing X Series 008. 

After four rounds the unlikely pairing secured the win via a unanimous decision.

The animosity between the two looked to have disappeared and a truce drawn.

However, in a bizarre and shocking turn of events, Deen the Great and Sharks ending up having a mass brawl whilst awaiting confirmation of the results, with their teams required to pull them both apart.

The mass brawl went viral and has since ignited several reactions and built much anticipation for their next meeting on October 14, 2023.
