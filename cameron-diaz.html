Cameron Diaz Hollywood Life

Bio:
Cameron Diaz (born Cameron Michelle Diaz in San Diego, California on Aug. 30, 1972) is a well-known actress who got her big break as a model in the early 90’s. Her acting career took off after a breakout role in 1994’s The Mask, along with follow-up roles in My Best Friend’s Wedding (1997) and There’s Something About Mary (1998). Cameron has hosted Saturday Night Live four times, including her most recent appearance in 2014. In 2002, she was nominated for “Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture” for her role in Gangs of New York.

Best Known For:
Cameron is best known for being an actress, including her role in 2000’s Charlie’s Angels and Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle in 2003.

Personal Life:
In Jan. 2015, Cameron married Good Charlotte‘s guitarist and singer, Benji Madden. Prior to her marriage, Cameron was involved in numerous high-profile relationship. The actress dated Alex Rodriguez, Justin Timberlake, Jared Leto, and Matt Dillon, just to name a few.
