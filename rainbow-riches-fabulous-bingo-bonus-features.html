How to play 

To place your bet, select the number of paylines you want in play.

There’s a maximum of 20 lines. As you win cash by spinning in a payline pattern, the more lines you have, the better your chances of winning.  

Next, select your bet per line. This is how much cash you bet on each of the paylines that you select to activate.

How to trigger a bonus  

There are three bonus games that you can activate: Road to Riches, Wishing Well and Pots of Gold.  

Road to Riches 

By spinning in three of the Road to Riches Scatters (which look like leprechauns), you’ll trigger the bonus feature.  

A leprechaun and his wheel of fortune appears before a path, leading up a rolling, green hill.

Click “spin” and the wheel turns. It can land on 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 or COLLECT.

If it lands on a number, that represents the number of steps up the winning path that you take.

You keep spinning until either you spin in COLLECT or you reach the end of the winning path and get the top prize, whichever comes first.   

All that walking wasn’t in vain! Your winnings pot is worked out as: Final position on the path x base stake. 

You can win up to 500x your base stake on the Road to Riches feature.

Ever made a cheeky wish at a wishing well? Rather than asking you to chuck on coin in, we’re hoping to help you pull LOTS of coins out of this bonus game.

Several wishing wells will appear on your screen. They each have a hidden value.  

When you pick one, its value will be shown.  

Your winnings pot will be worth: Value of the selected well x base stake.  

This is another chance to win up to 500x your base stake.
