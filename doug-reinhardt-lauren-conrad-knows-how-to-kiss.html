Lauren Conrad Knows How to Kiss!

Here’s a shocker. Doug Reinhardt, Lauren Conrad’s boyfriend on the upcoming season of The Hills, is kissing and telling.

These two actually go way back. They both lived in Laguna Beach (Doug’s sis Casey Reinhardt actually appeared on that show in a supporting role) and dated briefly during high school after being introduced by Lo Bosworth.

As for Stephanie Pratt’s claims to celebrity news tabloids last week that he and L.C. only dated for about “two weeks,” Doug Reinhardt, who’s hitting a miserable .201 over three years in minor league baseball, disagrees.

Doug Reinhardt insists his relationship with Lauren Conrad was “fairly serious.”

If that’s the case, whose idea was that so-called date between he and Stephanie Pratt, as shown in The Hills’ Season Four trailer?

“Let’s just say it wasn’t mine,” Doug says.

As for his love life now, he’s currently “single and ready to mingle.”

Looks like Lauren Conrad feels the same way: She’s been spotted spending QT with My Boys actor Kyle Howard. More on that a little later.
