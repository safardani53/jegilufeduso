LIVE'S Kelly Ripa has ripped viewers for their behavior but was scolded by the show's producer not to do that.

The moment happened during the Live with Kelly and Mark Inbox segment on Wednesday's show.

Kelly, 53, and her husband and co-host, Mark Consuelos, 52, revealed the answers to a poll they conducted about which direction people face in the shower.

The results showed that 65 percent of viewers faced away from the showerhead, while only 35 percent faced toward the showerhead.

This shocked Kelly, who immediately mocked those who admitted to facing toward the shower head when bathing.

"35 percent of our audience are sociopaths?" the talk show host said, looking off camera.

Executive producer Michael Gelman then shut down Kelly's claims.

"I wouldn't say that, Kelly. It's just an article from the Internet," the producer reasoned.

"Well, you know who you are," the Hope & Faith actress said directly into the camera before moving on to the next poll.
