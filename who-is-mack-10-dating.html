Dedrick D’Mon Rolison, better known by his stage name Mack 10, is an American rapper and actor. He has sold nearly 11 million records independently combining his solo and group works. Mack 10 made his first appearance on Ice Cube’s 1994 “Bootlegs & B-Sides” compilation on the remixed track “What Can I Do?” and was a member of hip hop supergroup Westside Connection along with Ice Cube and WC.
