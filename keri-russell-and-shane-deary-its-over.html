Keri Russell and Shane Deary: It's Over!

Keri Russell and Shane Deary are calling it quits after nearly seven years of marriage, the actress’ rep says. The couple separated over the summer.

Actress Russell, 37, and Deary, a carpenter, married on Valentine’s Day in 2007. They are parents to son River, 6, and daughter Willa, 23 months.

In other Keri Russell news, she had a bit of scare earlier this week when her Brooklyn Heights townhouse was burglarized while she was asleep.

"Everyone is fine, and Keri sends her thanks to the police," her rep told Us on Wednesday of this week. "She returned to work on The Americans today."

The NYPD says two suspects were charged with burglary and criminal possession of stolen items including a Filson handbag, a laptop, necklace and earrings.

We’re glad she was unhurt in that breakin and wish the couple the best in this tough time regarding their split. That can’t be easy on the kids or the adults.
