Home Depot is selling a $198 two-tone privacy fence with lock into rails feature and no paint or sta

A PRIVACY fence is selling for under $200 from Home Depot, and it’s made from low-maintenance materials.

The hardware store is selling the Pro-Series Woodbridge Privacy Fence Panel from the brand Veranda for just $198.

Measuring six feet high and eight feet wide, the fence is two-toned as it’s made with both white and tan vinyl.

Each fence panel is made of durable, UV-resistant vinyl so there is no need to paint or stain making it low-maintenance.

Plus, it’s backed by a lifetime-limited warranty.

While it comes unassembled, the tongue-and-groove pickets slide into place locking into the rails without glue or screws.

Each box comes with one complete panel, top and bottom rails, and pickets.

Shoppers have given the fence 4.5 out of five stars.

Home Depot gives customers 90 days to return the fence however, it's wise to look over the policy before buying.

Customers can order it online for free delivery or pick it up at their local store.

THINGS TO NOTE

The Veranda fence does not come with posts, post caps, or other hardware needed so those must be purchased separately.

Each privacy fence complies with the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission’s (CPSC) safety barrier guidelines for residential pools.

Just be sure to check with your local pool codes to make sure that this complies with any local regulations.
