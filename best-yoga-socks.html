The 7 Best Yoga Socks 2020 | The Sun UK

YOU'VE got your yoga mat, you’re kitted out in your finest gym attire — but why, oh why, are your feet so slippery?

Yep, yoga is a sweaty business and there’s nothing worse than losing your grip on the mat, mid warrior two.

Normal socks are a total no-go of course, so that’s where yoga socks come in — they have all the grip you need to stay focused on your practice while allowing your toes to feel completely free.

It’s the life hack you never knew you needed!

The benefits of yoga for both body and mind are more evident than ever, with more and more of us incorporating it into our everyday routines.

Unsurprisingly, that means there’s a LOT of choice these days when it comes to yoga socks. Luckily for you, we’ve sifted through the contenders to bring you the very best options to guarantee no slipping — whether you’re a complete beginner or an expert yogi.

1. Best toeless yoga socks: Gaiam's Toeless Yoga Socks

  • Gaiam’s Toeless Yoga Socks, Skyline, £13.99 from John Lewis - buy here

Gaim has over 25 years’ experience in the yoga industry, so it’s a great place to start when shopping for yoga socks.

These striped socks are available in a blue or grey colourway and feature targeted traction zones for a no-slip grip on any surface.

Best of all, the open toe design allows your toes to spread, giving you the best of both worlds with grip and freedom.

2. Best full-toe socks: ToeSox's Full Toe Bellarina Yoga Socks

  • ToeSox Full Toe Bellarina Yoga Socks, £13.99 from Yoga Studio - buy here

While toeless yoga socks are great for complete toe-wriggling abandon, there are some occasions when you might want to keep your feet completely covered, whether that’s due to colder temperatures or simply wanting to protect your feet in communal gym spaces.

These full toe yoga socks by ToeSox are made from organic cotton, perfect for allowing your feet to breathe, and feature a patented non-slip grip to help you keep your balance in your yoga flow.

3. Best print yoga socks: ToeSox's Low Rise Full Toe Yoga Socks

  • ToeSox Low Rise Full Toe Yoga Socks, £11,99 from Yoga Studio - buy here

Another great choice from ToeSox, this eye-catching pair feature a beautiful palm tree sunset motif to remind you of the beach while you enjoy your yoga practice.

There’s nothing more calming than the sea after all!

These are great for adding some subtle style to your yoga attire, while the low rise fit hugs your foot below the ankle to ensure no slipping.

4. Best long yoga socks: Lululemon's Evolution Leg Warmer

  • Evolution Leg Warmer, £34 from lululemon - buy here

Mindful movement brand Lululemon is a brilliant go-to for sustainable yoga gear, with a varied selection of styles and accessories.

Unlike some longer styles which include grip design, Lululemon’s leg warmer socks are designed for colder studios, with an open toe and heel that lets you grip your mat without sliding.

Wear them high or scrunch them low to bring that 80s Fame-aesthetic to your yoga class.

5. Best ankle yoga socks: Sweaty Betty’s Pilates Socks

  • Pilates Socks, £12 from Sweaty Betty - buy here

Cult fitness brand Sweaty Betty has been ensuring that 'style meets performance' in women's activewear for over 15 years, so it’s another essential place to shop for yoga socks.

While the title of these socks may say ‘Pilates’, this pair is also ideal for yoga, with a low-cut heel and strap across the foot to keep you secure.

Made in a super-soft and breathable fabric, the blue marl finish will look stylish on the mat, too.

6. Best glam yoga socks: SHASHI's Star Glitter Mesh Non-Slip Sock

  • SHASHI Star Glitter Mesh Non-Slip Sock, £16.90 from Amazon - buy here

Ok, we know yoga is all about calming the mind and strengthening the body… but how amazing are these glitter mesh socks by SHASHI?

Proving that a touch of glam does indeed belong in your yoga flow, these pretty socks bring new meaning to the phrase ‘twinkle toes’ — while still providing that all-important, anti-skid grip.

While your toes are twinkling in triangle pose, the COOLMAX moisture-wicking fabric will also keep your feet cool and dry.

What’s not to love?

7. Best sustainable yoga socks: Panda Paw Fit's Bamboo Non-Slip Yoga Socks

  • Panda Paw Fit Bamboo Non-Slip Yoga Socks, £34.99 from Amazon - buy here

If you're looking to shop more consciously, bamboo fabric is a brilliant eco-friendly and sustainable option to look out for.

Made from the bamboo plant, not only can it feel like soft cotton, but it's naturally hypoallergenic, moisture-absorbent and quick drying — making it perfect for yoga!

This pair by Panda Paw Fit feature traction grips in a cute panda design and come with top reviews from Amazon shoppers.
