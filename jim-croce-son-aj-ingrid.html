Who is Jim Croce's son AJ Croce?

AJ Croce was less than a year old when his famous father died in a tragic plane crash.

However, he has followed in his father's footsteps to complete the music legacy that Jim Croce started.

Who is Jim Croce's son AJ Croce?

AJ Croce was born on September 28, 1971, in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania.

AJ is a singer and songwriter who followed in the footsteps of his father, Jim Croce, to establish himself in the world of music.

During the early days of AJ's career, he used to perform at parties and nightclubs either as a sideman or band leader.

However, his career took a turn for the better when Ron Goldstein and Peter Baumann of Private Music signed him to his first record contract when he was 19 years old.

Subsequently, he went on to record two albums for Private Music - A. J. Croce and That's Me in the Bar featuring Ry Cooder and David Hidalgo.

Over the course of his career, he has released other albums and singles.

It should also be noted that AJ also owns a record label, Seedling Records.

What is AJ Croce's net worth?

According to Net Worth Post, AJ Croce has an estimated net worth of approximately $1.7million, as of June 2022.

His main source of wealth is said to be from his music career.

Who is AJ Croce's father Jim Croce?

Jim Croce was born on January 10, 1943, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Jim Croce was a folk and rock singer and songwriter.

During the early days of his career, Jim released five studio albums and numerous singles between 1966 and 1973.

However, his career took a turn for the better after he formed a partnership with songwriter and guitarist, Maury Muehleisen, in the early 1970s.

Indeed, his breakthrough came when his third album, You Don't Mess Around with Jim, was released in 1972.

Unfortunately, his career was short-lived after he died in 1973.

At the peak of his career, Jim Croce was set to release the lead single to his fifth album, I Got a Name, on September 21, 1973.
