Who is Alanis Morissette? | The US Sun

ALANIS Morissette is famously known for her hit singles Ironic and Head Over Feet.

The Grammy Award winner saw her mainstream breakthrough with her third album Jagged Little Pill.

Who is Alanis Morissette?

Born on June 1, 1974, Alanis Morissette is a Canadian singer-songwriter and musician from Ottawa, Ontario.

Both of Alanis' parents are educators; her mother Georgia Feuerstein is a high school principal and her father Richard Morissette is a French teacher.

In April 1991, she released her self-titled debut album after signing a record deal with MCA Records Canada.

In June 1995, Alanis released her most successful project to date; Jagged Little Pill.

Jagged Little Pill spawned the hit songs You Oughta Know, You Learn, Head Over Feet, Ironic, and Hand in My Pocket.

The record went on to sell over 33 million records worldwide.

Jagged Little Pill also earned her the distinction of Canada's first artist to achieve a double diamond album certification.

Alanis' commercial success continued well into the 1990s and beyond, earning seven Grammy Awards out of 14 nominations.

On June 17, 2022, she released her 10th studio album The Storm Before the Calm.

What is Alanis' net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Alanis' riches are estimated to be $60 million.

While speaking about her legacy, the music superstar told Rolling Stone: "The thing I always default to is that I'll always be here to write songs.

"What I love for my music to be able to provide is just this intimacy, and it’s an invitation for our humanity.

"There’s this whole current of what it is to be human that is overlooked by culture. Enter music.

"Music is this giant allowance for whatever messy, gorgeous, luminous, terrifying thing that’s going on. It’s like a permission button."

She added: "I think love and anger and pain are energies that move worlds.

"They open things up, they start the currents moving again if something’s stuck."

Who is Alanis married to?

On May 22, 2010, Alanis tied the knot with Mario "Souleye" Treadway in a private wedding ceremony at their home in Los Angeles.

The couple first met one another at a meditation gathering in the abovementioned city in 2009.

"So happy to share with you that my man Souleye and I got married," the singer shared with her Twitter followers.
