A WOMAN who hates wearing bras revealed her go-to items when she decides to support her chest.

The content creator, who wears a cup size 36C, modeled the different styles for her followers.

TikTok user Emma (@ehalst) explained: "I hate wearing bras so here's what I can force myself to put on some days."

In the video, the TikToker showed her viewers a bra from Urban Outfitters.

Emma said: "They are see-through, they are mesh, they do have an underwire but that’s it.

"This is completely sheer, there’s nothing here. I think that’s why I enjoy them, [it feels like] nothing is on," she added.

She also showed her audience a similar bra from Urban Outfitters in a light green shade.

For her third item from the bran, Emma held up a dark orange underwire bra.

"This one isn’t as see-through as the other two," the content creator said.

Emma also shared her appreciation for an underwire bra from Aerie.
