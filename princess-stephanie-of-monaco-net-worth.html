Who is Princess Stphanie of Monaco and what is her net worth?

PRINCESS Stéphanie of Monaco is the daughter of legendary Hollywood actress Grace Kelly.

The princess is famous for her charity work and her love life. Read on to find out more.

Who is Princess Stéphanie of Monaco?

Princess Stéphanie of Monaco is the youngest child of Rainier III, Prince of Monaco and Grace Kelly.

She is the youngest of the couple's three children and is 14th in the line of succession for the throne in Monaco.

She has worked as a singer, swimwear designer and fashion model.

She was educated in Monaco and Paris, receiving her French Baccalaureat in 1982.

Princess Stéphanie is the patron of the Grand Prix de Gymnastique de la Ville de Paris and is the president of several associations.

These associations include the Monaco Youth Centre and Princess Stephanie Activity Centre.

The princess is heavily involved in charity work, creating the association Fight Aids Monaco in 2003.

On October 6th 2006, Princess Stephanie was named a Special Representative for UNAIDS.

What is Princess Stéphanie's age?

Princess Stéphanie was born Stephanie Marie Elisabeth on February 1, 1965.

She was born in Prince's Palace in Monte Carlo, Monaco.

What is Princess Stéphanie's net worth?

Princess Stéphanie earns an impressive amount each year through her work in the fashion industry as a designer and charity work.

Her net worth is estimated to be around $100 million.

Is Princess Stéphanie married?

Princess Stéphanie has been married twice but is now divorced.

She has been called the wild child of Monaco in response to her tumultuous love life.

She had dated Paul Belmondo, Anthony Delon and Rob Lowe before being in a relationship with her bodyguard Daniel Ducruet in 1992.

The couple were divorced on October 4, 1996.

She began a relationship with a married elephant trainer Franco Knie in 2001.

However, that relationship came to an end in 2002.

Stéphanie's second husband was Portuguese acrobat Adans Lopez Peres, a member of Knie's circus ensemble.

They were married on September 12, 2003, but were divorced by November 24, 2004.

Does Princess Stéphanie have any children?

Stéphanie has three children from two relationships.

Her first two children were with her first husband, Daniel Ducruet, Louis was born in 1992 and Pauline was born in 1994.

Her youngest child, Camille, was born in 1998. Stéphanie did not initially name Camille's father when she was born.

But it was believed that her father was Head of Security Jean Raymond Gottlieb, Camille has acknowledged him as her father.

Because Stéphanie and Jean Gottlieb never married, Camille is not in the line of succession like her siblings as illegitimate children in Monaco cannot be in the line of succession.

How was Princess Stéphanie involved in the car crash that killed Princess Grace Kelly?

On September 13, 1982, Princess Grace Kelly died in a catastrophic car crash.

Princess Stephanie, who was 17 at the time, was in the car with her.
