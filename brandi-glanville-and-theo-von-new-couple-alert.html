Brandi Glanville and Theo Von: New Couple Alert!

It looks like someone out there is a fan of Brandi Glanville’s chiseled abs.

Because we assume there’s no way anyone is a fan of her personality.

According to E! News insiders, the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills cast member is dating the podcast host and former MTV reality star.

They met by appearing as guests on each other’s podcasts, with a source telling the site that the "romantic aspect" of their relationship is pretty new.

Glanville and Von allegedly went on a number of dates in private before making a public stop this past Tuesday night at the Los Angeles premiere of Natural Born Pranksters.

"He travels a lot because of his comedy schedule but they were spotted at his show last night holding hands," the insider said of Glanville and Von.

"So far, it seems like they’re really into each other."

Brandi, of course, is famous to those who watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills online or on television.

Von, meanwhile, recently released a Netflix comedy special titled "NO OFFENSE" and former starred in a season of MTV’s Road Rules, along with four seasons of the Real World/Road Rules Challenges.

"I think it’s amazing that you are trying to bring parachute pants back all by urself!" Glanville Tweeted at Von earlier this week, clearly flirting online and adding:

"it’s a bold&special move!I’m proud of U."

AWWWW! Don’t these two just make your heart melt?
