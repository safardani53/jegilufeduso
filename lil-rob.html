Who is Lil Rob:

Lil Rob is a famous Rapper. He was born on September 21, 1975 and his birthplace is Solana Beach, CA. Lil is also well known as, Born Roberto Flores, he is a Mexican rapper and actor, known for such singles as ‘Summer Nights’ and ‘Bring Out the Freak in You.’ He released his ninth studio album, Love & Hate, in 2009.

Lil is originated from United States. He had a small role in the film Dirty, starring Cuba Gooding Jr.
