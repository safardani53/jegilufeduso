How To Watch The 2024 Super Bowl

You can watch Super Bowl LVIII on any device by streaming it on Paramount+, or you can watch the traditional broadcast on CBS using cable, satellite, or a digital antenna.

Ready for the big game? That's a question that Vince Lombardi Trophy-coveting NFL players and coaches ask themselves each season. Well, you better get ready. Super Bowl LVIII will be here faster than you think.

Here's everything you need to know about how to watch and stream the 2024 Super Bowl live with Paramount+, as well all the details on when and where Super Bowl LVIII will be played, and who will broadcast the game. How to Watch the Super Bowl

You can stream the Super Bowl on CBS from any device with Paramount+. With Paramount+, the game can be streamed on a number of supported devices, including smart TVs, mobile devices, tablets, desktops, streaming devices, and gaming consoles.

The traditional Super Bowl broadcast will be available in two formats: CBS will carry the conventional broadcast of the game, and Nickelodeon will air an exclusive family-friendly telecast. The CBS broadcast is available on Paramount+, and both broadcasts are available on any cable or satellite service that offers CBS or Nickelodeon.

When is Super Bowl 2024?

The 2024 Super Bowl is scheduled to be played on Sunday, Feb. 11, 2024, at 6:30 p.m. ET. The date will mark the 22nd time, and the 21st consecutive time, that the Super Bowl has been played in the month of February.
 

What channel is the Super Bowl on?

CBS is airing the 2024 Super Bowl, and it is streaming it live on Paramount+. Super Bowl LVIII will mark the culmination of the NFL on CBS' 64th season. It will also mark the 22nd time CBS has broadcast the big game, the most of any network. CBS' history with the Super Bowl dates back to Jan. 15, 1967, when it broadcast the Green Bay Packers' victory over the Kansas City Chiefs in Super Bowl I.

How can I watch the Super Bowl on my phone or mobile device?

You can stream the Super Bowl from your phone, tablet, or Android device with the Paramount+ app.

The Paramount+ app is available on the Apple App Store or Google Play.

 

How to watch the Super Bowl without cable?

You can watch the 2024 Super Bowl without cable by tuning in CBS on a TV with a digital antenna. You can also watch the Super Bowl live without cable by streaming it on your screen or device with Paramount+.
 

Who will play in the 2024 Super Bowl?

The 2024 Super Bowl will be played by the winner of the AFC Championship Game and the winner of the NFC Championship Game. The Kansas City Chiefs are the reigning AFC champions and the reigning Super Bowl champions; the Philadelphia Eagles are the reigning NFC champs.  

The 2024 AFC Championship Game and the 2024 NFC Championship Game are scheduled to be played on Sunday, Jan. 28, 2024.

As with NFL on CBS games all season long, and like Super Bowl LVIII, the AFC Championship Game will be available to stream live with Paramount+.

Who is announcing the 2024 Super Bowl?

Jim Nantz, Tony Romo, and Tracy Wolfson will call the 2024 Super Bowl for NFL on CBS.
