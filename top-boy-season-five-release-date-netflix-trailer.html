Top Boy season 5: Exact date new series will air confirmed by Netflix in gritty trailer

TOP Boy fans have been given a glimpse at the final season in a gritty new trailer.

The Netflix smash hit will return for a fifth and final outing early next month.

The new trailer highlighted how the series will focus on revenge, retribution and loyalty.

Stars Ashley Walters and Kane Robinson will once again reprise their roles as East London drug dealers Dushane and Sully.

Sully’s actions at the end of the last series have rewritten his business rules with Dushane.

As new shared problems arise, everything they've built comes under threat from forces outside and within their empire.

Can they coexist by the rules of the road they’ve lived by their whole lives in a world that is changing before them? Or can there only be one Top Boy?

The trailer sees tension building on the Summerhouse estate, with rioting kicking off and a police car being set on fire as chants of 'Let him out!" grow.

Ominously, as Ashley's character is filmed in profile as he contemplates his next step, the trailer ends with a voiceover: "I know what you're on, but that road don't end good."

Speaking about the final series, Ashley and Kane said: "While the journeys of Dushane and Sully have remained at the core of the show, the new characters that have entered the world of Top Boy have become a key part of the show’s legacy, representing each new storyline in a raw, authentic way.

"With all this being said, and staying true to our original goal, every story must have an ending and so season three will be our finale.

"A chance to come full circle and end the journey in the right way.”

Season five cast members also include Little Simz, Jasmine Jobson, Saffron Hocking, Adwoa Aboah and Araloyin Oshunremi.

Meanwhile Brian Gleeson and Barry Keoghan have joined the show for the final series.

Top Boy fans immediately took to X - formerly Twitter - to discuss the trailer.

One wrote: "Can't wait."

Another added: "Bruh Am seeing Stefan s*** is getting serious."

A third tweeted: "Suspense killing me."

Top Boy season five will premiere on Netflix on September 7.
