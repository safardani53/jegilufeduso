Wheel Of Fortune host Pat Sajak drops a clue on who he'd pick as his successor as fans think star is

WHEEL of Fortune’s Pat Sajak has hinted at which TV personality he might like to replace him as host.

The America’s Game presenter, 76, made the unlikely suggestion amid rumors he is planning to retire.

On Monday November 7’s episode of Wheel of Fortune, Pat got to know the contestant Levy, who is a Navy veteran.

“Baking shows, you like baking shows?” asked Pat.

“I like to do that!” answered Levy.

The host enquired: “Do you like the British baking show?

“That’s one of my favorites!” Levy said.

Pat then surprised fans as he revealed: “Ahh, Paul Hollywood is my hero!”

The celebrity chef, 56, is a judge on The Great British Baking Show.

“We’ll talk over doughnuts!” gushed Pat as Levy smiled.
