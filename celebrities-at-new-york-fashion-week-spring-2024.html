Celebrities at New York Fashion Week Spring 2024

It’s the most wonderful time of the year … for the style obsessed. 

New York Fashion Week has kicked off, and the biggest names in Hollywood and beyond have stepped out to bask in the newest trends. Brands including Ralph Lauren, Michael Kors, Tory Burch and more are showcasing their spring/summer 2024 designs in the most posh venues across the Big Apple. 

The New York Public Library hosted the Coach presentation, which was nearly upstaged by an animal rights protest as Jennifer Lopez and Anna Wintour sat front row. Two  female activists with Peta stormed the runway, carrying a sign that read “Coach: Leather Kills.” The protestors were quickly whisked away by security and models resumed walking down the runway.

The festivities commenced on Thursday, September 7, and will conclude on Wednesday, September 13.
