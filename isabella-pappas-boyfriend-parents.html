Isabella Pappas Boyfriend Is From Showbiz But Not Parents

Isabella Noemi Pappas, better known as Isabella Pappas, is a London-based actress who grew up on a farm in northern Italy.

Her path into the world of entertainment began on the famed stages of London, where her exceptional ability garnered her nominations for prestigious awards such as the Laurence Olivier and WhatsOnStage Awards.

She’s become a famous figure on television thanks to her compelling portrayals, most notably in the ITV series Finding Alice in 2021 and her recurring part in the Disney series The Villains of Valley View (2022-present).

Let’s take a closer look at the Pappas’ life – discover her age, parents, boyfriend, net worth, and more!

Isabella Pappas’ movies and TV shows

Pappas, with a budding career in both television and gaming, has showcased her versatile talents in various roles over the years. In 2016, she made her debut in the miniseries Paranoid, portraying Sarah Waingrow in two episodes.

Her voice acting skills came to the forefront in 2017 when she lent her voice to the video game Horizon Zero Dawn as Young Nora. In 2019, she appeared in the TV series Almost Never as Rosie, featuring in the episode titled The Single.

The year 2020 saw her in a one-episode stint on Home, where she portrayed Gemma. Pappas’ breakthrough moment arrived in 2021 when she assumed the main role of Charlotte Walsh in Finding Alice.

Continuing her success, she currently stars in the Disney series The Villains of Valley View as Amy Madden/Havoc, a role she’s held since 2022. It has an IMDb rating of 5.6 out of 10.
