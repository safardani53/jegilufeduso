Morgan M. Shepherd is a semi-retired American professional classic racer and team owner. The last time he played part-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, he drove an 89th Chevrolet Camaro for Shepherd Racing Ventures. He currently owns Shepherd Racing Ventures and Landon Cassill as a full time driver. He is a reincarnated Christian who serves as a lay pastor for the racing community. He competed at NASCAR for over 50 years, having one of the longest careers in the sport. Shepherd became the second oldest race winner in 1993, when he won the spring race in Atlanta at the age of 51, 4 months and 27 days. He holds the record for the oldest runner who started racing in the Sprint Cup race at Camping World RV Sales 301 in 2014 at the age of 72
