Oscar Awards 2013: Memorable Quotes From the Red Carpet and Show

Going off-script! Hollywood's biggest stars shared some of their favorite moments from a busy year in film (and talked about getting ready for the huge industry affair) on the Oscars red carpet on Sunday, Feb. 24. Tinsel town's biggest bold-faced names, including Channing Tatum, Kerry Washington and Quvenzhane Wallis, weren't shy about sharing their excitement for the big day as they got pumped for a fun-filled night with host Seth MacFarlane.

Check out some of the stars' most memorable quotes from the red carpet and show!

"I can't wait to ride my electric scooter, walk my dog, watch TV, be bored … and I'm gonna put [my Oscar] in my hand and kiss it every night before I go to sleep."
— Quvenzhane Wallis to the AP about what she'll do after the awards season

"I'm glad that I get to take my final walk as the princess of 2012. …there's nothing wrong with winning an Oscar!"
— Octavia Spencer, last year's Best Supporting Actress winner, to the AP

"I can't even talk about it, I'm a puddle!"
— Jennifer Garner to ABC on being excited for her husband Ben Affleck's nominations for Argo

"Probably Sally Field, because she's turned into my favorite person in the entire world because she sends me such funny emails."
— Jennifer Lawrence to ABC about who she's looking forward to seeing

"This reminded me of 'Happy birthday Mr. President.'"
— Jessica Chastain to ABC on her stunning Giorgio Armani copper-toned gown

"I sewed most of the sequins on myself."
— George Clooney, on girlfriend Stacy Keibler's gown 

"We're going to have a lot of amazing documentation to show whatever it is that comes out, to be like, 'You were right there, man.'"
— Channing Tatum to ABC on enjoying the big night with his pregnant wife Jenna Dewan-Tatum
