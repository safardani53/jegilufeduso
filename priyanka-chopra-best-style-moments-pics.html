Priyanka Chopra Jonas Best Style Moments: Pics

Seriously chic! Since Priyanka Chopra Jonas stepped into the spotlight, she has blessed Us with some pretty stellar style moments. 

The Love Again star, who is married to Nick Jonas, doesn’t stray away from fun and funky colors, bold patterns or out-of-the-box silhouettes. She plays with hemlines and jewels, walks with ease in seriously high stilettos and serves up serious glam. 

From her time promoting Quantico and red carpet appearances, to strutting around the streets of New York City and London, the actress never fails to provide fashion inspiration. 

The Bulgari ambassador told Savoir Flair in April 2021 that she’s always been “interested in fashion,” but makes a point not to let the trends influence her attire. 

“I would never be able to tell you what is Spring 2021 or Fall 2021 of any designer. I leave that to the experts who work with my, my stylist. But I do like pretty things,” she admitted. 

And while walking down a red carpet is something she’ll love forever and always, she’s not a huge fan of all the ins and outs it takes to pull the whole thing off. “The process of getting to the red carpet has always been tedious for me,” she said. “I’m not a fan of glam. But I love being on the red carpets; I always have such a great time and I don’t take them too seriously.” 

Most recently, Chopra Jonas commanded attention at the inauguration of the Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre in Mumbai in April 2023. The Matrix Resurrections star stepped out in a sparkly sheer gown by Elie Saab that showed off her legs. The dramatic number featured a cape adorned with protruding flowers and a sequin-covered bodice. The looked was completed with a completely see-through tulle skirt. Chopra Jonas teamed the ensemble with a smoky eye, a warm lip and had her hair styled in a voluminous updo.
