Who is Tilian Pearson?

Tilian Pearson was born on July 12, 1987, in Clearwater, Florida.

Tilian is a musician, singer, songwriter, and record producer.

He is well-known for being a vocalist for the popular rock band, Dance Gavin Dance.

Subsequently, he joined Dance Gavin Dance in August 2012 and has been performing with the band to date.
