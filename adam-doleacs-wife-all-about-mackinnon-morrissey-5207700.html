All About MacKinnon Morrissey Hollywood Life

Adam Doleac has been a rising star in the country music world for a few years. After his breakout single “Whiskey’s Fine” blew up, he got to collaborate with huge names in that scene, including Darius Rucker and Kane Brown. In September 2022, he released his debut album Barstool Whiskey Wonderland with hits like “Girl in Love” and “Famous.”

As he continues to make waves in the country world, Adam is also set to perform at the Christmas at the Opry special on NBC on Thursday, December 7. When he takes the stage at the iconic country music venue, his wife MacKinnon Morrissey will undoubtedly be cheering him on from the sidelines. The rising country star has been wed to his wife for just over a year now.

Before Adam rocks the house for Christmas at the Opry, get to know more about MacKinnon and his love story with her.

How Did Adam Meet MacKinnon?

Adam revealed that he recalled his first meeting with his wife in the opening verse of his song “Coulda Loved You Longer” in a TikTok video in August. “We were both downtown, different sides of the same crowd/Wasn’t even gonna go out or stay that late/You wrote your number on a napkin without me even askin’/Still feels like it happened just yesterday,” he sings. While the singer didn’t divulge specifics, the verse makes it sound like the pair met during a chance encounter when they hit the town for a night of fun with friends.

MacKinnon Is a Senior Market Manager

MacKinnon’s Instagram bio states that she’s a Nashville native, and it lists her company as The Home Edit. She’s a senior market manager for the company, which helps create organizational tools for individuals with both products and techniques.

They’ve Been Married Since December 2022

The Barstool Whiskey Wonderland singer-songwriter revealed that he’d popped the question to MacKinnon in February 2022. He popped the question at the Franklin Theater in Franklin, Tennessee, after telling her that she’d be seeing her favorite movie. “I was definitely feeling a little nervous waiting on her to get to the theater, but the best kind of nervous,” he told People. “I had been planning this night for months. I was just so excited, and she couldn’t have walked through those doors fast enough.”

The pair tied the knot 10 months later in a ceremony in Santa Rosa Beach, Florida in December 2022, after five years together, via People. MacKinnon said it was special to get married in Florida, as the pair had spent many vacations there together.

They Have an Adorable Puppy

The pair are also puppy parents to a black dog, named Remi. MacKinnon has occasionally shared photos of her on her social media, and they even had a cocktail named after her at their wedding. They even put the pup’s face on the cups.

They’re Expecting Their First Baby

Just about one year after getting married, Adam and MacKinnon shared some more exciting news: they’re going to have a baby! The couple announced that MacKinnon is pregnant with their first child in a statement on December 5, 2023. “Ever since we met and been together in our relationship, we’ve always wanted to have kids for sure,” he told People. “So that part wasn’t a surprise, but it did happen a little quicker than we thought it might, but we’re both really excited it did.”

MacKinnon also said she’s been excited about welcoming their first baby. “I’ve always wanted to be pregnant, wanted to have kids, always been envious of like moms with pregnant bellies, friends with pregnant bellies. But It is wild, just every day you wake up and you’re like, ‘Wow, I’m growing a human,'” she told the outlet. “It’s been really awesome and I can’t really complain.”
