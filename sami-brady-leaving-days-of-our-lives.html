Is Sami Brady leaving Days of Our Lives?

NBC'S Days of Our Lives has been airing episodes since 1965.

However, over the course of more than five decades, a number of cast members have come and gone.

Is Sami Brady leaving Days of Our Lives?

The fictional character of Sami Brady was born on October 16, 1984, along with her twin brother, Eric Brady.

Sami's last airdate on Days of Our Lives is Friday, July 15, 2022, according to Soap Dirt.

This week's set of episodes have been particularly rough for Sami, as she thought she would remarry, but instead left her lover, Lucas Horton (played by Bryan Dattilo) at the alter after he was accused of kidnap and murder.

Sami decided to seek comfort in her ex-husband, EJ DiMera (played by Dan Feuerriegel), only to find him in bed with her younger sister, Belle Black (played by Martha Madison).

After EJ suggested that Sami and he get back together, she quickly, and wisely, blew him off.

Following all the drama she has endured, it is unknown where she will end up and if she will come back to Salem sooner than later.

Who plays Sami Brady in DOOL?

Alison Sweeney was born on September 19, 1976, in Los Angeles, California.

The 45-year-old is an actress, reality show host, director and author best known for her role as Samantha "Sami" Brady on the NBC soap opera, Days of Our Lives.

Sweeny's first appearance on the show was back in January 1993.

Throughout her time on the show, Sweeney earned a Daytime Emmy Award nomination, four Soap Opera Digest Awards and a Fan Voted Daytime Emmy Award.

She has also appeared in a number of Hallmark movies including Second Chances and the channel's movies & mysteries film series: Chronicle Mysteries.

What is Sweeney's net worth?

Sweeney's net worth is estimated to be $9million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The outlet reports that she made 45,000 per episode while hosting NBC's original, The Biggest Loser, from 2007 to 2015.
