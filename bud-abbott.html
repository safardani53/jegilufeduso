Who is Bud Abbott:

Bud Abbott is a famous Comedian. He was born on October 2, 1895 and his birthplace is Asbury Park, NJ. Bud is also well known as, Actor and comedian best known as one-half of the burlesque comedy duo Abbott and Costello. He and his comedy partner Lou Costello are best known for their immortal “Who’s On First?” routine, which they first performed in the 1940 film One Night In The Tropics.

Bud is originated from United States. Comedian Jerry Seinfeld claimed The Abbott and Costello Show to have served as an inspiration for his own sitcom, Seinfeld.
