George Jung (Criminal) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

George Jacob Jung, nicknamed Boston George and El Americano, is a former American drug trafficker and smuggler who was a major figure in the cocaine trade in the United States in the 1970s and early 1980s. Part of the Medellín Cartel , which is responsible for up to 90% of the cocaine smuggled into the United States. He specializes in large-scale cocaine smuggling from Colombia. The story of his life has been depicted in the biopic film Blow, starring Johnny Depp as Jung. Jung was released from prison on June 2, 2014, after serving a sentence of nearly 20 years for drug trafficking.
