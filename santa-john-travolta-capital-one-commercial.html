Who plays Santa in the Capital One commercial?

CAPITAL One is a banking company known for often working closely with big-name celebrities.

During night one of the 2023 MLB World Series Championship, Capital One aired their latest commercial starring someone they've partnered up with before.

Who is Santa in the Capital One commercial?

Capital One and the star of several major motion pictures, John Travolta, have paired up in the past - first releasing a commercial with the Grease actor in 2021.

Travolta has appeared in several ads suited up as Santa Claus.

On Friday, October 27, 2023, Travolta and Capital One dropped their latest collaboration.

Mimicking the opening credits of the 1977 hit Saturday Night Fever, Travolta's Kris Kringle struts down the streets of New York in a fitted red outfit - not Santa's typical suit.

The commercial, like the film, also features the Bee Gees' classic, Stayin' Alive.

Travolta played Tony Manero in the movie.

How can I watch the Capital One commercial?

Since airing on television, the Capital One commercial with John Travolta is available to watch on YouTube.

There are two versions: one is 30 seconds, and one that is a full minute.

What is John Travolta's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, John Travolta has an estimated net worth of around $250 million.

Travolta has appeared in dozens of movies and television shows throughout his lengthy career.

His first role came in 1972 when he was featured as an extra in an episode of Emergency!, a popular first-responder show following a fictional rescue squad at the Los Angeles Fire Department.
