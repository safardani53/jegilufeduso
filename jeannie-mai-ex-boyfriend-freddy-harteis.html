Who is Jeannie Mai's ex-husband Freddy Harteis?

JEANNIE Mai Jenkins announced in September 2021 that she is expecting her first child with husband Jeezy.

Mai's ex-husband Freddy Harteis appeared to slam the television presenter after her baby announcement.

Who is Freddy Harteis?

Harteis is Mai's ex-husband. They were married from 2007 till 2018.

He is an actor, known for 2019 film, The Maverick and the 2011 flick, The Hollywood Hunter.

He is engaged to Linsey Toole, and they share two children, Emersyn Rose, 2, and Huck Fredrick, 1.

What did Freddy Harteis say about Jeannie Mai's pregnancy announcement?

The Real co-host Jeannie Mai announced on September 20 that she and her husband Jeezy are expecting their first child together.

Jeannie told Women's Health that she never wanted children her entire life, however, falling in love with Jeezy made her see life differently for herself.

She described their love as "honest, pure, and safe…something (she) hadn’t felt as a child."

Harteis reportedly replied to a comment on an Instagram post thread.

The original post said, "Her first husband prob biting bricks and screaming rn [laughing emojis]."
