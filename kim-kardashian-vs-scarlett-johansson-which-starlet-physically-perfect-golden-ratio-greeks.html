Kim Kardashian Vs Scarlett Johansson: Which Starlet Is ‘Physically Perfect’?

Hold on, so you’re telling us that Kim’s itty bitty waist, plump bootie and perky boobs don’t hit the spot? They do for most men in the world but Scarlett, 30, is considered to be leading the pack in the world of Hollywood, according to a study conducted by Bluebella, a lingerie company that used the ancient Greek Golden Ratio. This ratio used to be the ultimate system used to determine the most beautiful women by using their measurements!

Scarlett scored a 96.4 percent, while Kim came in a 96.3. Okay, so she got beat by a hair but guess who came in behind? Helen Mirren, 69, with a 95.6 score! The Golden Ratio takes the measurements from your breast to your waist, your hip to waist and breasts to hips. Once those are recorded they are calculated using the Golden Ratio equation and anything closest to 100 is perfect.

But how did researchers come across these statistics? Bluebella commissioned the research because they were looking into various sizing options for their upcoming season. They were looking at celebrities and they stumbled upon the Golden Ratio and that’s when they discovered that all of the female celebrities they were looking at fell within a +/-10 range, according to the Daily Mail.

“We do a lot of research to make sure that our lingerie fits perfectly and that we understand trends around body shape,” founder Emily Bendell said. “We were running a computer simulation on celebrity women and in doing so we inadvertently discovered that Scarlett’s figure is pretty much as close as you can get to the perfect Golden Ratio.”
