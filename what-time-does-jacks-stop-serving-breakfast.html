What time does Jack's stop serving breakfast?

Jack's offers breakfast items seven days a week.

From Mondays to Saturdays you can find their breakfast menus from 5am to 11am.

On Sundays, it is available slightly later, starting from 6am to 11am.

Jack's was founded back in 1960 in Homewood, Alabama by Jack Caddell, per Alabama Live.

The burger joint started off with 10cent sodas, 15cent french fries, and 20cent milkshakes.

The fast food chain has grown ever since its debut and continues to serve customers with yummy menus.

Where is Jack's located?

There are more than 200 Jack's locations throughout the South.

They are only located in four states: Alabama, Mississippi, Tennessee, and Georgia.

If you want to find an exact location near you, you can use their website to enter your zip code.

Jack's Family Restaurants Inc. CEO Todd Bartmess told The Business Journals: "We want to be seen as a growing brand and not just somebody who's standing still.

"We want to be a great regional player in the Southeast where really our brand resonates with our guests and sort of the Southern lifestyle."

"If you don't have good food, then — and you're a restaurant company — that's a big problem," Bartmess added.

"So, we do have great food and quality food that we think is better than most of the other competitors."
