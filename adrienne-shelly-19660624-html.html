Adrienne Levine, known as Adrienne Shelly, is an American actress, director and screenwriter. She is known for her roles in independent films like Incredible Truth and Belief by Hal Hartley. She wrote the screenplay, co-starred and directed the movie Waitress, which was released later in 2007. On November 1, 2006, Shelly was found dead, hanged in a shower in her studio. The initial examination of the scene did not reveal any suspicious details and police appeared to believe it was a suicide. Her husband insisted she would never end her life and went back and forth to check the bathroom for suspicious shoe prints. Police arrested Diego Pillco, a 19-year-old construction worker from Ecuador who confessed to killing Shelly and made it look like she had committed suicide.
