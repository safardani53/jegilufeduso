Sister Wives' Kody Brown begs ex Christine Brown for 'counseling' as she snaps 'I'm done' in intense

SISTER Wives' Kody Brown has begged his ex-wife Christine to attend counseling with him in an intense sneak peek.

Ahead of tonight's all-new episode, TLC shared a tense scene between Christine, 51, and Kody, 54.

During the nearly three-minute clip, the exes attempted to repair their broken relationship.

Kody offered break-up counseling, but his former partner laughed at the idea.

He said: "Well, all I need is grief counseling. Listen, I need to get to a place where I don't hate you.

"So I never speak bad about you to my children."
