Dolph Lundgren Biography

Dolph Lundgren is a 65 years old Swedish actor, screenwriter, director, producer, and martial artist. He is best known for appearing in movies like Rocky IV. Apart from his acting career, the ex-husband of Anette Qviberg is also an engineer who received his chemical engineering degree from the Royal Institute of Technology.
