The Kardashians reveal first look at season 4 of Hulu show despite fans demanding the boring s

THE Kardashians have revealed their first look at Season 4 of their Hulu show despite fan complaints.

Toward the end of the third season finale of The Kardashians, viewers watched the post-credits season. 

 Kim Kardashian, 42, said to the camera: "We're back! Better than ever!"

After squealing with her sister Kylie Jenner, 25, Khloe Kardashian, 39, added: "This is my happy place."

As Kourtney Kardashian, 44, looked upset while shaking a watermelon, Kim continued: "More fun. More family. More fame. And much more drams."

The teaser ended with Khloe screaming at the camera.

THE FAN UPROAR

The fourth season is scheduled to return to Hulu on September 28. 

Unfortunately, viewers have been petitioning for the cancellation of the family show since the end of the second season.

The biggest complaints about Season 2 stemmed from the "boring" storyline, which included Kylie's second pregnancy, Kourtney's wedding with Travis Barker, and the family’s attendance at the 2022 Met Gala.

There was barely any mention of Kim's former relationship with Pete Davidson, 29, or reference to serious issues, such as the tragedy at Astroworld.

Critics speculated momager Kris Jenner, 67, exerted her power as an executive producer as she had the final say as what does and does not make the cut of each episode.

THE BACKLASH

Critics also slammed the family for making the program feel like an extended ad for the clan's many products and endeavors instead of showing their real-life dramas.

One upset viewer said: “Kardashians is such a chore to watch lately.

"It’s just constant ads and product placement."

Another upset viewer added: “There’s no fun anymore. There’s no wholesomeness or genuine sibling conversations. It feels all rehearsed."

A third person summarized: “There’s no reference to what is really going on in their lives - it’s just ads.”

FILMING SEASON 4

In May, Kim, 42, appeared to be filming the fourth season when she visited New York with cameras following her every move.

While in the Big Apple, the reality star shopped in the flagship store of Tiffany & Co. as the cameramen focused on doing their job.

While Kim shopped around, she was spotted by photographers wearing a dangerously low skin-tight bodysuit.

The suit zipped up in the front as she showed off an eyeful of cleavage.
