Madison Beer Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Madison Beer (born March 5, 1999 in Jericho, New York) is an American singer. Singing since she was a little girl, she first gained notoriety Justin Bieber linked a video of her singing Etta James‘ “At Last” on Youtube. After gaining attention from his fans, Justin took Madison under his wing by personally signing her to his record company, Island Records, and shares his manager, Scooter Braun, with her. He has since become her “mentor.” Following her exposure, Madison collaborated with Cody Simpson on the track, “Valentine,” but it was never officially released. It wasn’t until 2013 that she made her solo musical debut, “Melodies.” Since then, she has been in the process of recording a solo album and has released four other singles, one featuring rap duo Jack & Jack.

Best Known For:
Madison is best known for her association with Justin Bieber and her music career.

Personal Life:
Madison is currently dating Jack & Jack’s Jack Gilinsky. They started seeing each other and 2015 and appear to still be going strong.
