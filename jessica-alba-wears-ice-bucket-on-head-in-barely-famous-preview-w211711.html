Jessica Alba Wears Ice Bucket on Head in 'Barely Famous' Preview

Chamillionaire would be proud! Jessica Alba and Erin Foster have a little too much fun during a karaoke party in Barely Famous‘ season 2 premiere on Wednesday, June 29, as seen in Us Weekly‘s exclusive sneak peek.

The VH1 series’ preview clip shows the ladies performing an unusual rendition of Chamillionaire’s 2005 hit “Ridin’,” and mayhem ensues.

During the performance, Alba twerks, tosses her hair, twirls a jacket over her head and then wears the ice bucket like a hat, as one does.

At one point, Foster, 33, has a bone to pick with the song’s lyricist.

“It says the same thing so many times,” David Foster‘s daughter laments about the tune’s prompter.

Later, Alba lies down on the club’s booth seat while clutching a bottle of booze and screams, “Let’s go to Denny’s!” Who can argue with that?

Barely Famous stars sisters Erin and Sara Foster and satirizes the reality show genre. Among the famous folks appearing this season include Chris Martin, Chelsea Handler, Kate Hudson, Zach Braff, Kate Upton, Cindy Crawford, Brooke Burke, Dr. Phil, Ali Larter, Lauren London and Joey Fatone.

Alba, 35, rose to fame in TV with the starring role on Fox’s Dark Angel but is best known for such films as Sin City, Fantastic Four and Valentine’s Day. She has made recent appearances on the TV series The Spoils of Babylon, Comedy Bang! Bang! and RuPaul’s Drag Race.

Watch the raucous clip in the video above to see how Foster reacts to the Honest Company cofounder dumping all of the ice on her.

Barely Famous airs on VH1 Wednesdays at 10 p.m. ET.
