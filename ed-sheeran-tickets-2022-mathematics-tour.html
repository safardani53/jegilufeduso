Ed Sheeran tickets on sale now for 2022 Mathematics Tour - extra seats added to huge tour

ED SHEERAN has added extra tickets to all his Mathematics Tour dates.

All dates will be on general sale today, and so if you missed out first time around now is your chance.

Ed Sheeran is adding extra tickets to all shows for his Mathematics Tour, so if you haven't already got your tickets now is the time.

Tickets will go on sale April 29 at 10am from Ticketmaster and AXS - so set your alarms now.

The hottest ticket in town this summer, Ed Sheeran's Mahematic's Tour will see him play huge venues including Manchester's Ethiad Stadium and London's iconic Wembley stadium and many more.

However, he has cancelled his show on June 2 in Sunderland due to a scheduling conflict but fans can move their ticket to the Friday or Saturday gig (also in Sunderland), or receive a refund.
