Who is Jax Jones and what is his net worth? The US Sun

SONGWRITER, producer and DJ Jax Jones is making a BIG name for himself.

The 35-year-old has collaborated with some of music's top artists including MNEK, RAYE and Mabel.

Who is Jax Jones?

Jax Jones, born Timucin Fabian Kwong Wah Aluo, is a 35-year-old music producer and musician.

He has been building up his ability as a beatmaker from the age of 15, when he learned how to use software system Cubase.

Before starting to work with other artists, Jax got his name out on the music scene by appearing in open mic nights.

He is now well known for his collaborations with the likes of Demi Lovato, MNEK and Mabel.

What songs is Jax known for?

Jax's best tracks include a feature on the 2014 trace I Got U by Duke Dumont.

In late 2016, the song You Don't Know Me made him a radio favourite and featured singer Raye.

He has also appeared in song Breathe with Ina Wroldsen, and Instruction with Demi Lovato.

Over the years, Jax has had loads of successful collabs, but he revealed two artists the would still love to collab with.

Speaking on PlantRadio, he said: "I would love to make a song with The Weeknd or Billie Eilish. I just think, for dance music to do a record with those kind of voices would be really powerful and fresh, and push the genre forward.

"Especially House music because sometimes House music can be a bit nostalgic, so it's really interesting to try those sort of vocalists on it."

What is Jax's net worth?

It's believed he has a fortune of around £4M.

This is through the music he produces.

Jax has seen his bank balance swell since 2018.

Is Jax Jones married?

Not much is publically known about his wife, but they got married in 2017 in Thailand.

Jax told The Daily Star at the time: "I'm off the market ladies, I'm sorry. We got married out in Thailand in the mountains.

"We had water buffalos greeting the guests as they came in, and then the most amazing pool party as the after-party.

"Everyone ended up in the pool suited and booted!"

Does Jax Jones have any children?

In September 2020, the DJ and his wife welcomed a baby girl together.

He shared a string of snaps and revealed her unusual name.

"I wanted to share with you guys that I'm now a father to a beautiful baby girl called Lawana," Jax wrote.
