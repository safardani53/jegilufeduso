15 fabulous winter dresses for every occasion under 75

IT'S that time of year where the weather has turned and the evenings are bitterly cold, but that doesn't mean a good winter dress can't still fit the style bill.

From going out for festive drinks, to going into the office, a dress is a quick and easy way of pulling together an outfit without too much prep.

Obviously classic summer styles like strappy minis or tea dresses aren't going to cut the mustard in the colder months so we need to up the ante with cosy knits and turtle necks.

You may think that this means spending an arm and a leg on dresses that are genuinely cosy and feel great but what if we told you that there were loads out there for less than £75? If you wouldn't believe us then let us prove it.

So without further ado, these are the winter dresses you need to have in your wardrobe.

1. Nobody's Child Peggy Corduroy Mini Dress

Although this bright pink dress from Nobody's Child is, in fact, a mini, the thick corduroy makes it a beautiful winter option.

Long sleeves add a layer under your jacket and the high, rounded neck protects your chest from the elements.

To keep your legs warm, wear with a pair of black tights and long boots for any occasion - we particularly love this for a dinner out that calls for a relaxed but polished outfit.
