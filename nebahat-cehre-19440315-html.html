Nebahat Cehre (Movie Actress) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Hilal Nebahat Çehre, a Turkish-Turkish actress, model and singer who was crowned Miss Turkey 1960. She is best known for her role as villain Firdevs Yöreoğlu in the Kanal D Aşk-ı Memnu television series and as Hafsa Sultan in this Muhteşem movie Yüzyıl. As Miss Turkey, Çehre represented Turkey at the 1960 Miss World Pageant held in London, UK. She also represented Turkey in the 1965 Miss Universe Beauty Pageant held on July 24, 1965 at the Miami Beach Auditorium in Miami Beach, Florida, United States.
