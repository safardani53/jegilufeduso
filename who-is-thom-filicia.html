Who is Thom Filicia?

Thom Filicia was born on May 17, 1969, in Syracuse, New York.

Thom is an interior designer who is well-known for starring as an interior design expert on the television program, Queer Eye for the Straight Guy.

Additionally, he also co-authored a book based on Queer Eye for the Straight Guy.

Read More on The US Sun

He has also hosted several shows for the Style Network, including Dress My Nest and Tacky House.

Thom was a former student of Syracuse, where he graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in interior design.

He is also the founder of a design firm, Thom Felicia, Inc., in 1998.

Furthermore, Thom was a guest judge on The White Room Challenge.

Thom was also a contestant on Ru Paul's Secret Celebrity Drag Race in 2022.

However, he was eliminated after the third week.
