Finn Wolfhard Career

Finn Wolfhard began his career as an actor in 2014, appearing in a popular television series named The 100. This opportunity came from the well-renowned American classified website Craigslist.

Later, he appeared in another TV series named Supernatural. Then, he got the biggest break of all in 2016 when he was cast as the lead character in the famous Netflix series Stranger Things where he gained much popularity and became well-liked in the film industry.

He has been working on another animated series titled Carmen Sandiego Since 2019. Additionally, he has acted in some well-renowned films. He made his feature film debut as the lead in the movie It and It Chapter Two.

After that, he performed in other well-known TV shows and films including Ghostbusters: Afterlife, The Princess Bride, Aftermath, The Goldfinch, The Turning, Young Math Legends, Home movies, and Smiling Friends.

Then, he was featured in web series and music videos including Supermega, Headgum, Guest Grumps, Spendtime Palace, Weezer, Ninja Sex Party, etc. Wolfhard also acted in many famous podcasts including Lackluster Videos, If I Were You, and Review Revue.

Wolfhard co-star, co-directed, and co-wrote the dramatic feature film Hell of a Summer, which was released in 2023 with the help of his friend and old partner Billy Brick.

Throughout his career, he achieved many honors and nominations such as Guild Awards, MTV Movies and TV awards, Artist Awards, and the Atlanta Shortfest Awards for Best Director in 2020.
