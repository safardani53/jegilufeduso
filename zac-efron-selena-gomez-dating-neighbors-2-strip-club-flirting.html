Zac Efron &amp; Selena Gomez Dating? Their Flirty Night In Strip Club Hollywood Life

UPDATE: HollywoodLife.com has learned that there is no truth to the rumors of Selena and Zac’s flirty night out. “Selena wasn’t even in Atlanta on Sept. 3,” an insider told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. So, that’s that!

“They were having a blast and throwing dollar bills at the strippers,” Star magazine reports of the pair, who were in town filming Neighbors 2. “Zac asked one of the strippers to give Selena a lap dance and she was all for it. She nearly spit out her drink from laughing so hard.”

Uh-oh, this probably isn’t news the 27-year-old’s girlfriend, Sami Miro, wants to hear — especially since the couple celebrated their one year anniversary just two days later. “Zac was so touchy and playful with Selena all night,” the mag’s insider claims. “He tousled her hair and playfully poked her in the stomach. While we can’t deny that these two would be totally hot together, we have to remain a bit skeptical about these allegations. Selena is SUCH good friends with Vanessa, who famously dated her High School Musical costar for years before their 2010 split. And V has been there for the “Good For You” singer through her toughest times with Justin Bieber, 21, so we just can’t imagine Sel would betray that friendship. Plus, Zac and Sami are really serious and seem so in love!
