Who Is Allan Houston III? Allan Houston Son- Age And Wikipedia

Allan Houston III is the son of former American basketball player Allan Houston. 

While Allan Houston was a Knicks’ 1999 NBA Finals team member, his son is currently a wide receiver at Louisville.

Continue reading to discover more about Allan Houston’s son, including details about his age, and explore his background.

As the highest-scoring player in Tennessee’s history, Allan Houston was the 11th overall pick by the Detroit Pistons in the 1993 NBA Draft.

Throughout 12 NBA seasons, he earned All-Star recognition while playing with the New York Knicks in both 2000 and 2001.

Additionally, Houston also represented the United States National Team, contributing to their gold medal victory at the 2000 Olympics.

He is among the four Tennessee basketball players whose jerseys were retired, with his No. 20 ascending to the rafters in 2011.

In addition, In 2009, he was selected to the UT Basketball All-Century Team, and in 2018, he was inducted into the Tennessee Sports Hall of Fame.

Who Is Allan Houston Son?

Moreover, in 2019, he recorded a reception against Princeton on October 19, covering a distance of eight yards.

Unfortunately, in 2020, he couldn’t participate as all matches were canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Now, Allan Houston III is a wide receiver who’s spent four years at Brown University.

Joining Louisville, Houston becomes the fifth wide receiver to transfer ahead of Jeff Brohm’s inaugural season as head coach.
