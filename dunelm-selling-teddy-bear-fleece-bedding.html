GETTING out of bed in the morning is about to get a whole lot harder thanks to Dunelm.

The homewares company has launched a “teddy bear” fleece bedding set guaranteed to warm you up as the long nights set in.

According to the retailer the Teddy Bear collection “is crafted from sumptuously soft textured fabric for maximum comfort.”

The line includes pillow cases, fitted sheets and even duvet covers meaning that getting into bed is like getting a warm hug from, well, a teddy bear.

The collection is available in no less than eight colours including lavender and blush, meaning that there’s sure to be one to suit your taste.

And for those wanting something a little more adventurous there is also a leopard and star print set available.
