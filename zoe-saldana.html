Zoe Saldana Biography

Zoe Saldana was born on June 19, 1978, to Aridio Saldana and a Puerto Rican mother. Her birthplace is Passaic, New Jersey, but she grew up in Jackson Heights, New York. Saldana and her mother moved to the Dominican Republic when her father died in a car accident when she was nine years old.

Saldana has two sisters, both of whom are female. Mariel and Cisely are their names. She enrolled in the ECOS Espacio de Danza Academy. Her passion was to learn modern Latin dance, jazz, and ballet. She and her family moved to New York immediately after her sophomore year.

Career

Zoe Saldana was a member of the band Faces. During her tenure there, she was given the opportunity to appear on Law & Order in 1999. Zoe made her film debut as a dancer in Centre Stage. Saldana made her film debut in Crossroads in 2002, and she also co-stars in Drumlins with Nick Cannon.

In 2003, she played Anamaria in Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl. Her next role was as Dolores Torres in The Terminal.

Saldana appeared in Temptation and Haven the following year. In 2005, she appeared in Constellation, Dirty Deeds, and Guess Who with Ashton Kutcher.

In 2006 and 2008, she appeared in Premium and Vantage Point. She appeared in the films After Sex and Blackout in 2007.

Her big break came in 2009 when she landed two big deals. One played Nyota Uhura in Star Trek, and the other was the lead in Avatar.

The Losers, Death at a Funeral, Takers, Burning Palms, The Heart Specialist, and My Little Pony are among the others. She has appeared in Rosemary’s Baby, Nina, and Guardians of the Galaxy as Gamora.
