Photos of the Entertainer Hollywood Life

Marie Osmond is one of the most popular entertainers in Hollywood. See some of her best photos over the years in honor of her birthday on October 13.

Marie Osmond is one of nine children and the only girl. Marie’s brothers gained fame in the 1970s as part of a boy band that rivaled the Jackson 5 in terms of teen adoration. Marie had her own musical career as a country artist and as part of a duo with her older sibling Donny. The brother and sister had a successful variety TV series in the 1970s called, fittingly, ‘Donny & Marie,’ which sparked their signature tune, ‘A Little Bit Country, A Little Bit Rock ‘N’ Roll.’

Over the years Donny and Marie have continued performing together and even had an 11-year Las Vegas residency. Outside of her professional career, Marie (who married her first husband Steve Craig twice) had eight children – four girls and four boys. Sadly, her son Michael committed suicide in 2010 at the age of 18. Scroll through the gallery to see photos of Marie through the years.
