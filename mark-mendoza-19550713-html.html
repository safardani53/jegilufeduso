Mark "The Animal" Mendoza is an American bassist and member of the American heavy metal band Twisted Sister. He joined the group on October 31, 1978, after leaving The Dictators. He briefly performed in Blackfoot in the early 1990s. Mendoza is also currently a member of Joe Rock and the All Stars, contributing bass and vocals to this group with WBAB conductor Joe Rock on Long Island. He is also the co-founder of Area 22 Productions.
