Lindsay Lohan and Mean Girls co-stars reunite for hilarious new video- but fans are all asking the s

THE stars of Mean Girls have stunned fans by reuniting for a new ad, but fans couldn't help but notice one major missing element.

Lindsay Lohan, 37, reunited with Amanda Seyfried, 37, and Lacey Chabert, 41, to reprise their roles, but there was one major flaw in the nearly perfect reunion.

In the ad, Walmart reunited with the cast of Mean Girls to create an ad advertising their deals.

In a post shared on TikTok, Lindsay captioned the video: "Get in, besties. We’re going shopping. #BlackFridayDeals go live next week. #BlackFriday #Sponsored."

Opening the clip, Lindsay narrated over the present-day fictional North Shore High School, showing that there was a new group of "Plastics" running the campus.

Lacey, as Gretchen, the mom of one of the popular girls, shouted: "Get in, sweetie, we're going deal shopping!" as she held up her phone to show off Walmart's Black Friday deals.
