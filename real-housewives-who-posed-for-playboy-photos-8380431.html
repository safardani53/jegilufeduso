Real Housewives Stars Who Posed for Playboy: Photos

Stars of the various Real Housewives franchises have been everywhere, from daytime talk shows and Halloween movies to Fashion Week runways and even the pages of Playboy.

With season 13 of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills premiering on Bravo on Oct. 25, Playboy dipped into its celebrity archives to share photos of the Housewives stars who've posed for the brand through the years.

From a young Jeana Keough to a confident Garcelle Beauvais, take a look back at their stunning photos.

Denise Richards

Months after giving birth to daughter Sam Sheen, RHOBH's Denise Richards posed for Playboy in 2004. Years later, she told her about the shoot after Sam and sister Lola found out about it on their own.

“They heard that I was in Playboy. I didn’t even think that they would know what that is … [but] now kids can go on the Internet and see all kinds of things," Richards said on a 2019 episode of the Daddy Issues podcast. "They don't Google [me]. I don't think they want to know certain things, either, so they've heard bits and pieces. I'm honest with them."

Camille Grammer Meyer

Camille Grammer Meyer of RHOBH fame posed for several Playboy publications in her earlier modeling years, returning in 2011 (above) after she joined the Bravo franchise.

Garcelle Beauvais

"I really wanted to do it for me," Beauvais told Chris Yandek in 2007, the year she did the magazine's cover. "I think as you get older you're more comfortable in your skin. Definitely the hang-ups are a lot less than they were when I was in my 20s."

Lisa Rinna

RHOBH's Lisa Rinna posed for Playboy twice: first in 1998 when pregnant with daughter Delilah Belle Hamlin, and again for a The Graduate-inspired cover and spread in 2009 (pictured).

Joanna Krupa

RHOM's Joanna Krupa first appeared on Playboy in 2005, and again in 2009 (above) when she was on Dancing with the Stars.

"It's all [done] with taste, class," she told Access Hollywood. "It's very artistic. It's going to take Playboy to a level they've never gone before, so it's going to be really classy and beautiful."

Kelly Bensimon

When RHONY's Kelly Bensimon stepped in front of the cameras in 2010, she was photographed by none other than her ex-husband, famed photographer Gilles Bensimon.

"I'm thrilled to be on the cover and show what sexy looks like at 41," she told Page Six at the time.

Jeana Keough

In a 2008 story for Bravo, RHOC's Jeana Keough revealed that as a one-time Playboy model in 1978, "I still get my free issue for life every month."
