Dwayne Johnson Channels '90s 'Young Rock' for Christmas

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is having a bit of fashionable fun this holiday season!

On Monday, the multi-talented entertainer paid homage to his younger self by poking fun at one of his infamous outfits. 

“Merry Christmas, from your keepin’ it 💯 friend. 90’s Rock 🖤🎄👊🏾 🥃#TappinFannys 👋🏾,” 51-year-old captioned an Instagram video of himself replicating one of his style choices that previously went viral.

In the upload, the Moana star wore a black long-sleeved turtle neck, and denim jeans that he accessorized with a gold chain and black fanny pack. His signature bald head was covered in faux hair fit for the ‘90s.

As the video played, the former WWE star did his own version of the classic holiday tune “The Christmas Song (Chestnuts Roasting on an Open Fire).’”

“Chestnuts roasting on an open fire/ ‘90s Rock nipping at your nose/ I’m walking down the hall/ looking cool as f---/ just take one look at my amazing clothes/ Everybody knows/ a turkey and some camel toe, I mean, mistletoe/ helps to make the season bright/ although it’s been said, many times, many ways/ Merry Christmas, to you," he sang.

Johnson panned the selfie video across his clothing, taking a stroll through the home before stopping in front of a Christmas tree. 

He proudly showed off the outfit he can’t seem to outrun in a July 2014 Facebook post.

During an interview when he earned the title of PEOPLE’s 2016 Sexiest Man Alive, The Rock opened up about his past style choice. 

“Alright, so here’s the thing,” the Black Adam actor explained to PEOPLE at the time. “It takes a lot of confidence to rock a fanny pack. Put your thumb in the fanny pack. I’m giving a look.”

He added, “I’ve got a tissue underneath my elbow because I felt like my turtleneck was expensive. The funniest thing about this picture is, this is not a joke. I walked out of the house like this. Like: Hey baby, this is it, right?’”

The Rock’s outfit became a social media hit again when his friend and Jumanji: The Next Level costar Kevin Hart wore the ensemble as his 2019 Halloween costume.

“I killed it with my Halloween costume this year…. @therock had the nerve to get an attitude with me. 😂😂😂😂😂 ” the comedian, 44, captioned an Instagram video.  

In the clip, a group of kids who were trick-or-treating visited Johnson. Once the children got their goods, Hart showed up dressed as his friend.

“Why are you dressed like that?” Johnson asked. 

“Because it’s my costume! All you gotta do is go to the costume store and say, ‘Make me look stupid,'” Hart joked, striking the same pose seen in Johnson's old photo.
